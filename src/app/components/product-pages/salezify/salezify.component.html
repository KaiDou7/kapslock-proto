Our Features

Manage your inventory / items

Restock / Deduct items in the inventory as per the availability.

Create your own categories for items such as drinks , starters, main course etc.

Create / Edit / Delete items in the items panel.

Give the items name, unit, price as per your needs.

Generate reports easily as per the category or all at once.

Manage your orders

Add table number to respective orders.

Automatically sets date and time of the order.

Select the items ordered and the bill is automatically calculated with or without gst.

Edit / Delete an order at any time.

Print bills on one click once the order is closed.

Get quick reports

Get reports of your Inventory.

Use filters to generate reports of a specific day or a specific time span.

Available to download in Pdf or Excel format
