Our Features

01 Fast

Even though the apps are loaded with striking features we make sure to always keep our apps lightweight. Let’s be honest no one likes apps that load with the speed of a sloth.

02 Robust

Along with agility our apps are also built to be robust i.e. they are highly fault tolerant and prone to lesser crashes. Even in case of crashes we make sure your on-going data is preserved and kept safe.

03 Cross-platform

Our apps are built to be platform independent so you don’t have to worry about being either an android or an iOS user.

04 User-Friendly UI / UX

With app stores being loaded with similar apps we rely on our first impressions based on their design. That is precisely why we dedicate our time in curating the right UI for your users. User-engagement is a crucial factor of mobile apps and can be achieved with an easy-to-use UX. We employ a seamless onboarding experience for your users to ensure lesser abandonment and more engagement.

05 Accessible

Our apps are designed according to Web Content Accessibility Guidelines 2.0 to Non-Web Information and Communication Technologies (WCAG2ICT). This makes our apps accessible with various assistive technologies such as screen readers, allowing people with disabilities to join the target audience of our customers.

06 Platform Design Guidelines

We follow proper platform design guidelines laid down by both Google Play Store and Apple App Store so that your app doesn’t face any issues. We pay attention to all design and developer guidelines to carefully craft the details of your app and promise its longevity
