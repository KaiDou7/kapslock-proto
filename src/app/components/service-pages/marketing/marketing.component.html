Our Features

01 Understanding Your Goal

When you start social media marketing you start with a plan. To come up with a plan you need to research your target market and figure out who your audience really is. That way we can pick out specific crowds in specific locations and understand the market size. Once we have a defined market we tap it with carefully curated posts.

02 Target Audience Oriented Marketing and engagement

Depending on what your audience is like we design our posts around them, something that they will find attention grabbing and entertaining. The content we put out is to the point, engaging and one that raises curiosity so that your clients are drawn to know more. The inflicted curiosity can be used to get more traffic to your website raising brand awareness and initiating communications.

03 Gaining Loyal Customers

We want the brand image to be fun and trust worthy so as to have more loyal customers. Statistics show that 71% of consumers who have had a positive experience with a brand on social media are likely to recommend the brand to their friends and family. This is the kind of loyal customer base we wish to attain for you.

04 Strategy For More Success

Once you see a good traffic and conversion rate on your website it means your campaign is going well but that’s not it. We need to constantly keep analyzing our strategy and improving to keep the engagement live because we don’t want a bored audience or to be buried with several other ads. We need to keep your top position secured in the market.

05 Rewarding Customers

Speak with your audience often, ask them to take up polls or drop their opinions to keep the engagement going and making them feel valued. Reward them in the form of giveaways, freebies or competitions they can take part in and get new customers interested in your brand through these incentives. We will fully to design and run these fun campaigns for you.

06 Metric Trackers

We will help keep a track of all the import metrics such as likes, shares, follows, sentiments, post reach, post engagement, conversions, etc. via analysers and keep testing new marketing strategies for best results and conversion rates.
